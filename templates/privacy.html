Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how WonderFloyd collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit wonderfloyd.com or contact us. By using this website, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree with this policy, you should stop using the website.

1. Who we are

WonderFloyd is a digital space focused on science, technology, curiosity, consciousness, and related topics. The website is operated from within the European Union.

2. Data we collect

We try to keep data collection limited and reasonable. We currently collect the following types of information:

2.1 Information you provide directly

When you contact us through any form or by email, you may provide:

  • Name or nickname
  • Email address
  • Any information included in your message

This information is used only to read, respond to, and manage your communication.

2.2 Technical and usage data

When you visit wonderfloyd.com, standard technical information is logged automatically by the server. This may include:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of access
  • Visited pages and URLs
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Referring website (if any)

This data is used for security, basic analytics, and to keep the website running correctly.

2.3 Cookies and similar technologies

We currently use only technical and functional cookies that are necessary for the website to operate, such as:

  • Session or login cookies used for the administration area of the site

These cookies do not track your activity across other websites and are not used to build a personal profile of you.

At this time, we do not use advertising cookies or third party tracking cookies. If we add analytics or similar tools in the future, this policy will be updated and, where required, you will be informed or asked for consent.

3. How we use your data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the website
  • To ensure security, prevent abuse, and detect technical issues
  • To respond to messages and inquiries sent by you
  • To understand basic trends such as which pages are visited more often
  • To comply with legal obligations where required

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Legal basis under GDPR

Since WonderFloyd is operated from the European Union, the processing of personal data is based on the following legal grounds:

  • Legitimate interest: running and protecting the website, keeping server logs, preventing misuse, and understanding general site usage
  • Consent: when you choose to contact us and voluntarily provide your information
  • Legal obligations: where we must retain or share information to comply with applicable law

5. Data sharing and third parties

We may share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate the website or handle communications, for example:

  • Hosting providers and server infrastructure
  • Email service providers
  • Future analytics or monitoring tools, if added

These providers are allowed to process personal data only to the extent necessary to deliver their services and are expected to protect it according to applicable regulations.

We do not share your data with third parties for independent marketing purposes.

External websites that we link to or cite in the Sources section have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for how those sites collect or use data.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as it is needed for the purposes listed in this policy:

  • Messages and contact emails are stored for as long as they are reasonably useful for communication, record keeping, or resolving issues
  • Server logs are kept for a limited time for security and maintenance, then deleted or anonymised
  • If we are required by law to keep certain records for longer, we follow those rules

When data is no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymised where possible.

7. Your rights

If you are in the European Union or another region with similar data protection rules, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • The right to request deletion of your data, where applicable
  • The right to restrict or object to certain types of processing
  • The right to receive your data in a portable format, where technically feasible
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, you can contact us at:

contact@wonderfloyd.com

We may need to verify your identity before acting on your request.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You use the website at your own risk and should take care when sharing sensitive information online.

9. International transfers

Depending on the location of our hosting and service providers, your data may be processed in countries inside or outside the European Economic Area.

When data is transferred outside the EEA, we aim to ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent protections, as required by law.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your personal data is processed, you can contact us at:

contact@wonderfloyd.com
